# Demo Scenarios

## 1) AI Lead Qualification
- Scenario: Prospect qualification from website inquiries
- Example prompt: "We are a 20-person accounting firm serving SMEs in Belgium and want to automate website lead capture."
- Expected result: The system identifies lead qualification needs and suggests an AI lead assistant workflow.
- Business value: Better lead quality and faster sales follow-up.

## 2) AI Lead Qualification (Real Estate)
- Scenario: Filtering high-intent inquiries before calls
- Example prompt: "I run a real estate agency and want to qualify website inquiries automatically before scheduling calls."
- Expected result: Suggest lead qualification assistant and scoring workflow.
- Business value: Less time spent on low-intent leads.

## 3) AI Knowledge Assistant
- Scenario: Customer-facing conversion support
- Example prompt: "How can AI assistants improve lead conversion on business websites?"
- Expected result: Mentions instant responses, qualification questions, personalized interactions, and appointment scheduling.
- Business value: Improved conversion and response speed.

## 4) AI Knowledge Assistant (Internal)
- Scenario: Internal documentation search
- Example prompt: "What is an AI knowledge assistant for internal teams?"
- Expected result: Explains search across documentation, onboarding, and support knowledge.
- Business value: Faster internal answers and better consistency.

## 5) Automation Calculator
- Scenario: Estimating process automation potential
- Example prompt: Use quick-fill values `Company size: 15`, `Weekly manual hours: 35`
- Expected result: Automation potential around 41% and about 56 hours saved per month.
- Business value: Clear first estimate before discovery call.

## 6) ROI Calculator
- Scenario: Estimating financial impact from AI automation
- Example prompt: Use demo values in `/ai-roi-calculator`
- Expected result: Monthly hours saved, automation %, monthly savings, annual savings, and annual ROI.
- Business value: Faster business-case conversations with prospects.
